A letter to the members of LOQM. 

Hi, Archit here. 

Firstly, at the risk of sounding like a broken record, my apologies for the delay with which this was delivered to you. Punctuality is not my strong suit. 

Secondly, to the members of the lab, who I hope will be able to make use of this work. I was initially trying to make a GUI (with buttons and dialogs) for tcspc data acquisition and analysis as I thought it would be most convenient for you. 
HOWEVER, I realised it might be better to provide a CLI type of package so that 
    (a) It would be more friendly for you to look under the hood and try to fix it, if something were to not work (Trust me, snAPI fails quite annoyingly at times).
    (b) It is my personal opinion that experimental physics is a spectacular field because of the all-rounder skillset it equips you with, from an understanding of mathematical theory to the ability to make (more or less) anything you want to, for your experiment or otherwise. 
    Working with drivers, hardware and a CLI-like environment is a natural first step in that direction (if you haven't worked with these before, of course).

Finally, to Srivatsa, Amrita and Anshuman sir, thank you for being such lovely people. 

My best.
